NEON NEXUS MACROELECTRONIC MUSIC FESTIVAL IN LLEIDA

  • Starts at 8:00 pm.

  • May, 27, 2024

  • Street General Brito, 25007, Lleida, in Hotel Zenit.


Embark on a sonic odyssey through the pulsating heart of electronic music, where each beat serves as a gateway to euphoria.

Feel the exhilarating rush as the bass reverberates through your core, syncing your soul to the collective rhythm of the crowd.

Experience the transcendental fusion of sound and emotion, as melodies intertwine with memories, creating an indelible tapestry of bliss.

Unleash your inner adventurer as you navigate the labyrinth of soundscapes, guided by the expert hands of world-renowned DJs.

Join us under the starlit sky as we dance into the dawn, celebrating the universal language that unites us all: music.

DJs

At NeonNexus we are going to have famous DJs and breakout DJs from all tech subgenres, from tech-house, hard-tech, acid-house, and even electro.

Come see famous Djs like ADAM BAYER , CARL COX and DEBORAH DE LUCAH !!

Vibrate and enjoy the music with these DJs with whom you will be amazed!
