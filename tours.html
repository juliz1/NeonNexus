Based in Lleida


Our base is in Lleida, we are proud to create an immersive experience where music, community and creativity come together vibrating high.

From our modest beginnings we want to become a beacon on the electronic music landscape, we have maintained an unwavering passion for pushing boundaries and defying expectations.

We believe in the power of music to unite people from all walks of life, fostering a sense of belonging and shared joy. Our commitment is to continuously innovate and provide unforgettable experiences for our audience.

Our goal


Our goal is to offer you a temporary escape into a world where rhythm is the law and energy is infinite.

We bring together a carefully curated selection of world-renowned artists and emerging talents to take you on an unforgettable sonic journey.

But the Neon Nexus is more than just music; we strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone is welcome to be themselves, without judgment or prejudice with music as a universal language that transcends barriers and unites people in a shared experience of ecstasy and liberation.

Food, drinks and sweets


Step into a world of culinary delights at our festival, where we take pleasure in offering complimentary treats to all our cherished attendees. From the moment you arrive, tantalize your taste buds with a cornucopia of flavors, all available without spending a dime

And for those with a sweet tooth, our dessert stations are a paradise of confectionery delights. Indulge in an array of decadent treats, from rich chocolates to fluffy pastries, each bite a symphony of sweetness

Dive into Aquatic Adventures


Immerse yourself in a world of aquatic excitement at our festivals, where water becomes the ultimate playground for adults seeking thrills and relaxation alike. Picture yourself basking in the sun as you glide down winding water slides, or feel the rush of adrenaline as you brave the wild currents of our lazy rivers.

Come join us and make a splash at our next event. Leave your cares behind and dive into a world where fun knows no bounds and every moment is an opportunity for aquatic exploration and exhilaration.

Join us in our next chapter as we continue writing the history of techno, one note at a time. See you on the dance floor!
